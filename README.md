## ⚡ Using the software

The main window shows a list of new tokens detected on the network. You set your buying rules in the control panel.

### Defining buy rules
Choose the amount of Solana to spend on every trade. You also set the slippage tolerance. Slippage allows for price changes during the trade. A setting of 5% to 10% is common for fast trading.

### Using Telegram for control
You control the bot from your phone using Telegram. Create a new bot inside Telegram using the BotFather service. Paste the API key into the software settings. You receive messages when the bot finds a token. You reply to those messages to confirm a buy or sell action.

### Understanding Jito bundles
The bot uses Jito to send trades. This tech helps get your transaction into the next block. It makes your trades faster than standard methods. You pay a small tip to the network to prioritize your order.

## 🛡 Staying safe while trading

Trading new tokens involves high risk. Follow these practices to protect your data and balance:

*   Back up your private keys in a secure, offline location.
*   Monitor your trades through the Telegram logs.
*   Set a balance limit in the application. This prevents the bot from spending your entire wallet on one trade.
*   Ensure your internet connection remains active. A drop in signal stops the bot from catching new launches.
*   Check for updates regularly. Newer versions contain fixes for connection issues.

## 🔍 Frequently asked questions

### Does the software work on Windows 7?
No. The application requires Windows 10 or higher for full support.

### Is the bot free to use?
The application is available for download at no cost. You only pay the standard Solana network fees for your trades.

### Why does the bot skip some tokens?
The software filters tokens based on your active rules. If a token does not meet your criteria for liquidity or age, the bot will not trade it. Adjust your filters in the settings menu to change this behavior.

### Does the bot store my funds?
The bot does not hold your funds. Your funds stay in your wallet. The bot only sends instructions to move funds when you trigger a buy action.

### Can I run multiple instances?
You should run only one instance of the bot at a time. Multiple instances can cause errors with your wallet nonce and lead to failed transactions.

### How do I stop the bot?
Close the application window to turn off the bot. Any active trades that have not completed will process through the blockchain as normal. The bot does not keep your trades open once it stops.
